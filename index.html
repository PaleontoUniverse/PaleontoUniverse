BASICS

What are Dinosaurs?

Dinosaurs are a class of extinct reptiles and modern birds of the clade Dinosauria. They first appeared during the Triassic period, between 243 and 233.23 million years ago, although the exact origin and timing of the evolution of dinosaurs is not know and is under progress. They became the dominant terrestrial vertebrates after the Triassic–Jurassic extinction event 201.3 million years ago; their dominance continued throughout the Jurassic and Cretaceous periods. Dinosaurs wiped out during K-T or Cretaceous–Paleogene extinction event approximately 66 million years ago. Dinosaurs can therefore be divided into avian dinosaurs, or birds; and non-avian dinosaurs, which are all dinosaurs other than birds.

What are Time periods?

Dinosaurs lived in Period called Mesozoic. thus Mesozoic era was divided in 3 geologic periods, the Triassic ( 245-208 million years ago), the Jurassic (208-146 million years ago), and the Cretaceous (146-65 million years ago).

Are all Dinosaurs big?

NO!

Not all Dinosaurs were big, Dinosaurs were like Balloons, some were big, some were small, while some were averge sized for example: for large we have Amphicoelias fragillimus which is about 60 metres (200 ft) in length and with a mass of 150 tonnes (150 long tons; 170 short tons). Then we have Velociraptor which grew up to 6.8 feet (2 meters) long, 1.6 feet (0.5 meter) tall at the hip and weighed up to 33 lbs. (15 kilograms) or we have Compsognathus which grew upto 1 m (3 ft) long and probably weighed about 2.5 kg (about 6.5 lb).Thus, if someone sterotypes dinosaurs as big and huge just nod your head and ignore him/her.
